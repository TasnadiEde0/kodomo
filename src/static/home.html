Kodomo

One out of six couples have difficulty getting pregnant despite trying for more than a year. This situation can be hugely frustrating, especially for those women with irregular cycles who are trying to conceive. To help these women better know their fertile days, Kodomo has developed a solution that combines a simple saliva test with an artificial intelligence powered website.

Using computer vision and machine learning, the website helps women easily and more accurately identify saliva crystals. By identifying the crystals that correspond with their fertile window, women can improve their chances of conceiving.
